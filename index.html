Which Country has the most Innovation Potential?

Research & Development (R&D) expenditure is a convenient index to measure a country's innovation capacity.

Today, the US and China lead the world, both increasing their expenditure rapidly. But this is mainly due to the size and growth of the economy in both countries.

In terms of expenditure per GDP, these countries do not necessarily lead the world.

At the top of the per GDP expenditure, we have Israel and South Korea, both with heavy focus on emerging technologies and the tech industry.

Following up are Taiwan, Sweden, and Japan, all with strong tech industries.

Finland, the home of Nokia, has also had high percentage expenditures in recent years.
